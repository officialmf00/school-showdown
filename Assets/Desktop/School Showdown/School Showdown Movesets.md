**Mo:**



M1 = Mo throws a quick jab with a very subtle shock sound.



M2 = Mo throws another quick jab with a very subtle shock sound. However, this one uses his other arm.



M3 = Mo does a quick autocombo where he throws multiple quick shock producing punches. provided M1, M2, and M3 are pressed in quick succession.



S1/Charge Shock = Mo stands still and charges up a small electric explosion that goes off closely around him when released. The longer the move is charged up, the stronger it is.



S2/Quickflip = Mo thrusts into the air and flips forward, giving him iframes while doing so.



S3/Dashing Beam = If the input is held down, Mo will stand still and charge up a beam of electricity, that gains range/damage the longer it is charged up. However, if the input isn't held, Mo will dash forward and send out a quick shock on the last frame, stunning anyone caught in it.



S4/Diagonalite = If mid-air, Mo will shoot downwards in a right diagonal line, sending a line of electricity across the floor when landing. If Mo is on the ground instead, only the line of electricity will occur.



ULT/Rain Of Terror = Mo will launch forward in an attempt to grab his opponent. If he grabs them successfully, a cutscene will play where Mo grabs them by the face and slams them into the ground, dragging them across the floor. Once dragged enough distance, Mo will throw them into the air, proceeding to hit them with a barrage of lightning strikes. Afterwards, he will zoom behind them, and the cutscene will end with Mo sending out a substancial, seemingly gargantuan punch with a fade to white.
